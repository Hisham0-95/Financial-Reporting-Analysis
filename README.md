## Project Overview

This project simulates a complete real-world financial reporting cycle for Bravo Co., starting from raw business transactions and ending with an interactive Profit & Loss dashboard. The workflow covers the full accounting cycle — journalizing transactions, preparing financial statements, calculating KPIs, and visualizing performance trends across 2018–2020.

## Project Goal

To simulate a real-world financial reporting process using raw transaction data, demonstrating the ability to bridge accounting knowledge with data visualization skills — turning raw numbers into actionable business insights.

---

## Workflow

1. **Data Import & Modeling** — Define relationships and prepare data structure
2. **Journal Entries** — Record and classify business transactions in Excel
3. **Income Statement Preparation** — Build P&L using calculated fields
4. **KPI Calculation** — Gross Profit, EBITDA, Operating Profit, PBIT, Net Profit
5. **Time Series Analysis** — Revenue and margin trends across 2018–2020
6. **Dashboard Design** — Interactive filters by Country, Month, Quarter, and Region

---

## Key Insights

- **Strong Revenue Growth:** Sales grew from 3.69M (2018) to 8.01M (2020) — a 117% increase over three years, reflecting strong commercial momentum

- **Gross Profit Scaling Well:** Gross Profit grew from 2.38M to 5.34M across 2018–2020, maintaining a healthy growth trajectory in line with revenue

- **Operating Expenses Rising Faster:** Total operating expenses jumped from 1.64M to 3.82M — growing at a faster rate than revenue, driven mainly by Staff Costs (260K → 527K) and Marketing Commissions (290K → 824K)

- **Net Profit Declined in 2020:** Despite record revenue, Net Profit dropped from 1.30M (2019) to 1.29M (2020) — signaling that rising costs are beginning to erode profitability

- **Marketing Expense Outpacing Sales:** The Sales vs. Marketing Expense chart shows marketing spend growing disproportionately relative to sales growth — suggesting diminishing returns on marketing investment in 2020

- **EBITDA Still Strong:** EBITDA grew from 1.15M to 2.24M, indicating the core business remains operationally healthy despite rising bottom-line pressure
